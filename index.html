Welcome to Dark Academia

An academic aesthetic for higher education, the arts, and literature, or an idealised version thereof.


1 / 3
Dark Academia uses a lot of gothic architecture. The ancient yet unique styles have a lot of history. The aesthetic revolves around mostly European history.
2 / 3
Dark Academia is also centered around the museums. Arts and literature are the biggest concepts. Things like dates at a museum, staying in a library, and drinking tea by the window while it's foggy or rainy outside.
3 / 3
The aesthetic is inspired by the 1989 film, Dead Poets Society There are certain ways of thinking and attitudes towards education. People that enjoy the aesthetic desire to live and work in a place where it's foggy/rainy and can express their thoughts. Journaling is a popular hobby among the culture.
