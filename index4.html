Sometimes, on a day where the fog overlays the area, we'll also grab our shoulder bags and pack our ink and quill pen. We'll stroll to the museums where we are alone with the art and our thoughts. We'll find a spot and sit down.


Sometimes, we'll be practicing our calligraphy and notice how beautiful the night is. Then, we'll recieve a folded note soaked in Earl Grey black tea and carefully open it. The letter reads; "Meet me on in the fields behind the doors of our school? 8pm~" we don't know what's happening. We open our door and grab our bag and sneak out. We carry our oil lit lantern and look around for the mysterious person. Then we see another oil lantern and make our way over to them. After a brief handshake. We walk over to the field and lay down. Slowly drifting off.
